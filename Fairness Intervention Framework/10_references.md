# References

This playbook is informed by established research and industry practices in algorithmic fairness, causal inference, and responsible AI.

## Foundational Fairness Literature

- Barocas, S., Hardt, M., & Narayanan, A. (2019).*Fairness and Machine Learning: Limitations and Opportunities.*https://fairmlbook.org/

- Hardt, M., Price, E., & Srebro, N. (2016).*Equality of Opportunity in Supervised Learning.*https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.02413

- Kleinberg, J., Mullainathan, S., & Raghavan, M. (2017).*Inherent Trade-Offs in the Fair Determination of Risk Scores.*https://arxiv.org/abs/1609.05807

## Key Insight

This playbook synthesizes ideas from:
- causal inference (Pearl, Kusner)
- statistical fairness (Hardt, Kleinberg)
- optimization-based fairness (Zafar, Agarwal)
- industry tooling (Google, IBM, Microsoft)

into a unified engineering workflow.
