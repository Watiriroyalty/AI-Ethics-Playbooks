# AI Ethics Playbooks

This repository contains a collection of practical AI ethics playbooks developed to support the assessment, mitigation, and implementation of fairness practices in AI systems.

## Overview

The playbooks provide structured frameworks and actionable guidance for teams seeking to build more equitable, responsible, and trustworthy AI solutions.

## Included Playbooks

### 1. Fairness Audit Framework
A framework designed to assess AI systems for potential fairness risks and biases across the AI lifecycle. It provides guidance on identifying affected stakeholders, evaluating outcomes, and documenting findings.

### 2. Fairness Intervention Framework
A practical framework for selecting, implementing, and evaluating interventions aimed at reducing unfair outcomes in AI systems. It supports teams in moving from identified risks to measurable corrective actions.

### 3. Fairness Implementation Playbook
A step-by-step guide for embedding fairness considerations into organizational processes, governance structures, and AI development workflows. This playbook focuses on operationalizing fairness principles from design through deployment and monitoring.

## Purpose

The purpose of this repository is to provide practical resources that help organizations:

- Identify fairness risks in AI systems
- Design and implement effective mitigation strategies
- Integrate fairness considerations into AI governance and development processes
- Promote responsible and trustworthy AI practices

## Intended Audience

These playbooks are intended for:

- AI and Machine Learning Practitioners
- Data Scientists
- AI Governance and Risk Teams
- Product Managers
- Responsible AI Professionals
- Researchers and Students
